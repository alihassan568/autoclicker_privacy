# Privacy Policy for Pro Autoclicker

**Last Updated: December 27, 2024**

## Introduction

Pro Autoclicker ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for the Pro Autoclicker mobile application.

## Information Collection

**Pro Autoclicker does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

The app operates entirely on your device and does not:
- Collect personal information
- Track user behavior
- Send data to external servers
- Use analytics or tracking tools
- Access your contacts, photos, or other personal files

## Permissions Used

The app requires the following permissions to function:

### 1. Accessibility Service Permission
- **Purpose**: Required to simulate touch events on your device
- **Usage**: The app uses this permission solely to perform automatic clicks at locations you specify
- **Data**: No data from other apps is collected or stored

### 2. Display Over Other Apps Permission
- **Purpose**: Required to show floating control buttons and target indicators
- **Usage**: Enables the overlay interface that remains visible across all apps
- **Data**: No information about other apps is collected

## Data Storage

All app settings and configurations are stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Can be deleted by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app
- Is not backed up to any cloud service

## Third-Party Services

Pro Autoclicker does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.
